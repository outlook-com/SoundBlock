About The Product


Were you ever talking to your girlfriend/boyfriend and found parents eavesdropping?

Got a call in library?

HERE IS THE SOLUTION

SoundBlock is a mask-type wearable, which allows you to speak privately by blocking your sound from going outside. It has a very cool appearance. When you are not using it, you can wear it in your neck just like a headphone, giving you a very swaggy look. Whenever you get a private call or you don't want to disturb the people in your surroundings or you want the people around you to escape from your tuneless song, just wear SoundBlock on your mouth and enjoy your privacy.
